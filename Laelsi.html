About

This site is just a simple photo and video gallery dedicated to our 3 cats; Ruby, Alice and Remy. Ruby is the cream colored one with the most energy and biggest appetite despite being the smallest. Though she likes us, she generally does not care for other people unless she gets to know them. Alice is the big, fat, lazy grumpy cat of the three even though she hates all food except for her kibble for some reason. She also absolutely despises cats, especially the other two. Remy is the only male and our newest cat. He is usually friendly to everyone except for the other cats but he will bite if you pet him too much.
